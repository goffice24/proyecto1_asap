Waiter
Waiters or waitresses enter orders, serve food and beverages to customers, prepare accounts, and handle payments at food establishments, eg, restaurants, hotels, and clubs.
Housekeeping
The housekeeping person is responsible for keeping public areas, bedrooms and bathrooms in a clean and orderly manner, ensuring that guests have a clean and pleasant experience.
Installers
An installer is a person in charge of putting together everything you need for an event.
Barista
A barista is the professional specialized in high quality coffee, who works creating new and different beverages based on it, using various types of milk, essences and liqueurs, among others.
Dishwasher
The helper, kitchen assistant or dishwasher is responsible for washing dishes, pots and other kitchen utensils. Cleanup. Help keep all rooms in the kitchen in perfect cleanliness.
Bartender
A bartender is someone who serves licensed drinks behind a bar to customers. You can find them in bars, clubs, restaurants, among other similar places. They are in charge of mixing and serving drinks or what is called cocktails, most of them contain alcohol.
Cashier
Some of the functions of a cashier are to collect, to close the box, clear the box and safeguard it. A cashier is the one who receives and delivers money to users of a bank, an establishment or a store, and takes care of the cash register
Kitchen assistant
Help cooks prepare food (eg cleaning and peeling fruits and vegetables, cutting bread and meat). Prepare simple food such as sauces, dressings, sandwiches, soups, desserts, etc. Check stocks and inform supervisor when more orders need to be placed.
